ABOUT
ME.

Busy lifestyles mean we are constantly on the go, and this takes away fun from our everyday lives. Thus, when I design I like to add fun to functional because that is how life should be. I love designing beautiful and functional product designs, as I believe that living with beautifully designed products can bring us incredible joy, especially when they are a part of our daily lives. I am looking to make an impact.
